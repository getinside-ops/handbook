# Account, Team & Audiences

::: details How do I add a new user to my account?
Go to **My Organisation → Team** and click **+ Add a member**. Enter the email address and role. Recommendation: always keep at least one backup member to ensure responsiveness within 48h for campaign proposals.
:::

::: details How do I register a new audience?
Go to **Monetisation → Catalogue** and click **+ New audience**. Fill in the profile carefully: brand, persona, average order value. This information is what advertisers consult when selecting your audiences.

→ [Detailed guide: Audience Setup](/en/publishers/audience-setup)
:::

::: details How do I make my audience profile attractive to advertisers?
Advertisers browse the catalogue like a marketplace. To maximise your chances of receiving offers:

- Add visuals: photos of parcels, email screenshots, packaging examples.
- Describe your persona precisely: age, product category, purchase frequency, geographic area.
- Provide a realistic average order value (AOV) — a precise figure is more convincing than a range.
- Activate multiple formats: an audience offering Parcel Insert + Emailing is more attractive than a single-format audience.

→ [Complete guide: Audience Setup](/en/publishers/audience-setup)
:::

::: details Do I need to create multiple accounts if I have multiple sites?
No. A single account can host as many audience profiles as you have distinct sites or logistics flows. Each audience is independent: rates, available formats and opening periods are configured separately.
:::

::: details Does getinside operate internationally?
Yes, getinside is present throughout Europe. You can add European audiences and receive targeted campaigns for those markets from the same account.
:::

::: details How do I track my affiliation commissions?
The status of your referrals is visible in the **Affiliation** tab. You earn a recurring commission of **20%** on platform fees generated by their activity. Your earnings appear in **Transactions → Revenue** and can be withdrawn or reinvested from your Wallet.
:::

::: details Why has my Score Distributeur dropped?
The score is recalculated dynamically. Frequent causes: response time exceeding 48h, campaign refusals without a counter-proposal, distributed volumes below commitments, CPM too high relative to the competition.

→ [Complete guide: Score Distributeur](/en/publishers/score-distributor)
:::
