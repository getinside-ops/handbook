# Billing & Payments

::: details I don't see my revenue in my Wallet
The credit is not automatic upon signature, but upon **realization**. You must manually validate according to the format:

- **Parcel Insert:** Have you filled out your weekly volume declaration? (On Monday or Tuesday)
- **Email:** Did you click on **"Validate broadcast"** after sending?

Without this action, the funds remain escrowed.
:::

::: details How to withdraw my earnings and issue my invoice?
Go to **Transaction → Exchange** and click on `Withdraw`. Enter the amount.

**Important:** If management fees apply, issue your invoice accordingly for the net amount received.
:::

::: details When are the revenues available?
The campaign revenue is credited to your account balance **30 days after the last reporting**. Then choose `Transfer to wallet` or `Withdraw` your revenue.
:::

::: details When are payments issued?
Payments are issued **30 days end of month**, from the date of receipt of the withdrawal request, provided that the submitted invoice is valid.
:::

::: details Can I reinvest my earnings?
Yes, without fees. In **Exchange**, click on `Transfer to wallet`. You can use this balance immediately to buy traffic for your own site.
:::

::: details Why is my revenue blocked for 30 days?
The 30-day delay is a **protection period** that secures your payment and that of the advertiser.

It allows detecting and processing potential anomalies (lost boxes, non-compliant volumes, interrupted campaign) **before** the funds are definitively released. Without this buffer, getinside could not guarantee your payment in the event of advertiser default.
:::

::: details What happens if an anomaly is detected during the 30 days?
getinside contacts you directly to resolve the incident. During the resolution, the delay for the availability of funds is suspended. Once the anomaly is handled, the counter resumes.

In common cases (volume discrepancy, non-compliant flyer), the resolution takes 48 to 72 hours. Contact [support@getinside.fr](mailto:support@getinside.fr) if you don't receive feedback within 5 working days.
:::

::: details What are the contractual penalties?
- **Late campaign cancellation:** flat penalty of **550€ HT**.
- **Platform circumvention** (direct contact with a partner met through getinside): penalty of **2,500€ HT** per campaign concerned.

These penalties are set out in the General Terms of Use and Commercialization (CGUC).
:::

::: details Can my funds expire?
Yes. If you do not log in **and** do not withdraw your revenue for **12 consecutive months**, the available funds expire and are definitively acquired by getinside.

**How to avoid this:** log in regularly, or enable balance notifications in your account space. A partial withdrawal or reinvestment is enough to reset the counter.
:::
