# Billing & Payments

::: details My revenue is not showing in my Wallet
Credit is not automatic upon signing — it occurs upon **completion** and requires manual validation depending on the format:

- **Parcel Insert:** declare your volumes every Monday or Tuesday in **My Campaigns → Declare volume**.
- **Emailing (Dedicated or Display):** click **"Validate broadcast"** after sending, in **My Campaigns → Validate broadcast**.

Without this action, funds remain escrowed and the campaign is not accounted for.
:::

::: details From when is my revenue available?
Campaign revenue is credited to your account balance **30 days after the last reporting**. Funds move from "Pending" to "Available" status. You can then choose to transfer them to your Wallet or withdraw them.
:::

::: details When are payments issued?
Payments are issued **30 days end of month** from the date of receipt of the withdrawal request, provided the submitted invoice is valid and funds are in "Available" status.
:::

::: details How do I withdraw my earnings and issue my invoice?
Go to **Transaction → Exchange** and click **Withdraw**. Enter the desired amount. If management fees apply, issue your invoice for the net amount received.
:::

::: details Can I reinvest my earnings without fees?
Yes, without fees. In **Exchange**, click **Transfer to Wallet**. The balance is immediately available to fund your own acquisition campaigns.
:::

::: details Why is my revenue blocked for 30 days?
The 30-day delay is a protection period. It allows detecting and handling any anomalies — lost parcels, non-compliant volumes, interrupted campaign — before funds are definitively released. Without this buffer, getinside could not guarantee your payment in the event of advertiser default.
:::

::: details What happens if an anomaly is detected during the 30 days?
getinside contacts you directly to resolve the incident. The funds availability delay is suspended during resolution. Once the anomaly is handled, the clock resumes. In common cases (volume discrepancy, non-compliant flyer), resolution takes 48 to 72 hours. Contact [support@getinside.fr](mailto:support@getinside.fr) if you have not received a response within 5 working days.
:::

::: details What are the contractual penalties?

::: warning Penalties set out in the CGUC
- **Late campaign cancellation:** flat penalty of **550€ HT**.
- **Platform circumvention** (direct contact with a partner met through getinside): penalty of **2,500€ HT** per campaign concerned.
:::

:::

::: details Can my funds expire?
Yes. If you do not log in and do not withdraw your revenue for **12 consecutive months**, the available funds expire and are definitively acquired by getinside. To avoid this, log in regularly or enable balance notifications. A partial withdrawal or reinvestment is enough to reset the clock.
:::
