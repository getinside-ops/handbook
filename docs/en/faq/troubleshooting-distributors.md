# Troubleshooting — Distributors

Your Wallet doesn't match? A withdrawal is delayed? A campaign is causing a problem? Find the answer in less than 2 minutes.

## Wallet & Payments

::: details "My available balance is 0 even though I distributed campaigns"
The Wallet balance is **not automatically** credited after a distribution. You must validate manually according to the format:

| Format | Required Action | Where on the platform |
|--------|---------------|----------------------|
| 📦 Parcel Insert | Declare volumes every **Monday or Tuesday** | My Campaigns → campaign → Declare volume |
| 📧 Emailing (Dedicated or Display) | Click on **"Validate broadcast"** after sending | My Campaigns → campaign → Validate broadcast |

Without these actions, the funds remain **escrowed** — the campaign is not accounted for.
:::

::: details "My balance is 'pending', not 'available'"
**Cause:** The 30-day protection period has not yet elapsed.

Your earnings go through 2 states:
1. **Pending**: Distribution confirmed, 30-day period underway
2. **Available**: 30 days elapsed without incident → withdrawal possible

> **This is normal.** This delay protects everyone (including you) against post-distribution anomalies.

If your funds remain "pending" after 30 days → contact support with the campaign number.
:::

::: details "I haven't received my transfer"
**Check in this order:**

1. **Have you submitted an invoice?** → Without a valid invoice attached to your request, the payment will not be sent.
2. **Has the 30-day period passed?** → The balance must be "Available" (not "Pending").
3. **Is the IBAN on your account correct?** → Check in Settings → Bank details.
4. **Are you within the normal timeframe?** → Payment is issued **30 days end of month** after validation of your request.

If everything is in order and the transfer is still missing → contact [support@getinside.fr](mailto:support@getinside.fr) with:
- The date of the request
- The requested amount
- Your IBAN
:::

::: details "A 5% fee was deducted, I don't understand why"
The 5% fee only applies to the **Starter Offer** starting from the **2nd withdrawal of the calendar year**.

| Offer | Withdrawal fee |
|-------|-----------------|
| Starter | 1st withdrawal/year: FREE · Subsequent: 5% |
| Platform | All withdrawals: FREE |
| Managed | All withdrawals: FREE |

If you are on Platform or Managed and a fee was deducted → it's an error, contact support immediately.
:::

:::: details "Funds were deducted from my Wallet without my request"
::: warning Automatic penalties deducted from your Wallet
- **Cancellation of a validated campaign:** flat penalty 550€ HT + platform fees
- **No response within 48h to a proposal:** penalty applied according to contract
- **Late response without valid reason:** may result in a penalty
:::

Check the **Wallet History** section on the platform to see the details of each movement.
::::

:::: details "My funds have expired"
::: danger Expiration after 12 months of inactivity
If you do not log in AND do not withdraw your funds for 12 consecutive months, the available funds are definitively acquired by getinside. This rule is contractual.
:::

**Prevention:** Log in at least once a quarter, or schedule an annual withdrawal.

If you think the expiration is unjustified → contact support with your login history.
::::

## Campaigns & Operations

::: details "I accepted a campaign but I can no longer honor it"
Act **as early as possible** — penalties increase over time:

1. **Immediately contact** your account manager or support
2. **Do not cancel** from the platform without discussing it first
3. Explain the situation — an alternative solution is often possible (postponement, replacement by another distributor)

> The more you anticipate, the more you limit the penalties. A last-minute cancellation is more costly than an anticipated cancellation.
:::

::: details "I didn't answer a campaign proposal within 48h"
The 48h rule is contractual. If the deadline is passed:

- The proposal **automatically expires** and is sent to another distributor
- A **penalty may be applied** to your Wallet according to your contract
- Your **Distributor Score** may be impacted (reduced visibility on future campaigns)

**To avoid this in the future:** Enable email/SMS notifications from your Account Settings.
:::

::: details "A campaign is incomplete or the received visual/brief is incorrect"
1. **Do not** start the distribution without having checked the material
2. Report the problem via the platform (campaign profile → Report a problem) or directly contact your manager
3. getinside contacts the advertiser to correct — the distribution is paused during the correction
:::

## Account & Affiliation

::: details "My referral commissions are not paid"
The payment of affiliation commissions is **automatically suspended** if you have unpaid invoices with getinside. Regularize your billing situation to unblock the payments.
:::

::: details "My account is suspended or blocked"
Possible causes: unpaid invoices, non-compliance with platform rules, suspicious activity detected.

Contact support at [support@getinside.fr](mailto:support@getinside.fr) indicating your account email and the displayed error message.
:::

## Handling Entry Errors

To err is human. Here is how to correct your most frequent blunders without stress.

::: details "I made a mistake in entering my weekly volume"
Did you type 10,000 instead of 1,000? Don't panic.
1. **Do not attempt** to re-enter immediately if the system is locked.
2. Send an immediate message to the advertiser via the campaign messaging to let them know.
3. Contact [support@getinside.fr](mailto:support@getinside.fr) with the campaign number and the correct value. We will correct the data manually within 24 hours.
:::

::: details "I clicked 'Validate broadcast' too early or by mistake"
This action is normally irreversible as it triggers billing.
*   Notify getinside immediately (via chat or support@getinside.fr).
*   If the invoice has not yet been issued, we can cancel the action.
*   If it has been issued, we will have to issue a credit note and restart the process.
:::
