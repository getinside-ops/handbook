# Dedicated Email (Publisher)

Make the most of your available send slots by broadcasting dedicated campaigns on behalf of selected advertisers. It is the most remunerative format in the catalogue: a single send, designed in your brand style, recommending a partner brand to a segment of your base.

You stay in control of the editorial. You choose which advertisers reach your subscribers, you build the email in your visual identity, and you decide on the targeted segment. The advertiser provides the brief and visuals; you turn an open send slot into direct revenue.

::: tip For advertisers
Are you a brand looking to run this format? See the [Dedicated Email guide for advertisers](/en/advertisers/dedicated-email/).
:::
