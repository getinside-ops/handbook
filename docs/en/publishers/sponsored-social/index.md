# Social Ads (Publisher)

Generate incremental revenue by granting selected advertisers the right to boost your posts — without impacting your editorial line.

::: tip Guaranteed revenue
Your payment is credited to your getinside Wallet as soon as the campaign ends. The advertiser invests their own media budget on your content.
:::

## How it works

getinside manages the entire campaign on your behalf: advertiser selection, creative production, targeting and campaign management. Your role is limited to granting boost rights and validating the content.

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">1</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Grant boost rights</h3>
    <p>You authorise getinside to boost posts from your Instagram, TikTok or Facebook account via the platforms' native tools (Business Manager, TikTok Ads). Your editorial line is not touched.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">2</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Creative validation</h3>
    <p>You receive a preview of the campaign before it goes live and validate the content on the getinside platform.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num done">3</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Revenue credited</h3>
    <p>At the end of the campaign, your payment is automatically credited to your getinside Wallet.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Your role vs. what getinside manages

| Your role | getinside manages |
|---|---|
| Grant boost rights | Advertiser selection and coordination |
| Validate creative before delivery | Creative production and copywriting |
| Validate the campaign on the platform | Targeting, setup and campaign management |
| — | Tracking and reporting |

## Eligibility

This format requires an active Instagram and/or TikTok account with an engaged audience. Detailed eligibility criteria are defined with the getinside team during your onboarding.
