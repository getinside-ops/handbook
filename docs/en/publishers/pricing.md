### * Sliding scale detail (Platform Offer)

The Network Fee rate automatically adjusts based on the annual revenue volume generated.

| Total Annual Revenue | Network Fee Rate |
| :--- | :--- |
| from 0€ to 5,000€ | **30%** |
| from 5,000€ to 25,000€ | **25%** |
| from 25,000€ to 75,000€ | **20%** |
| over 75,000€ | **15%** |

:::tip Withdrawal fees
With the **Starter** offer, the first annual withdrawal is free, then a 5% fee applies. With the **Platform and Managed** offers, withdrawals are unlimited and free.
:::

:::info Funds availability & Wallet
Revenues are credited directly to your Wallet **30 days after the campaign ends**, following reporting validation. Payment is made by bank transfer within 30 days end of month upon your request.

The balance can be **reinvested without fees** to fund your own acquisition campaigns (Smart Funding).

⚠️ Any unwithdrawn balance after **12 consecutive months of inactivity** is definitively acquired by getinside.
:::
