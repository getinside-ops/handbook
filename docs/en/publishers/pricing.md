## Detailed comparison

| Services | Starter (Free) | Platform (499€ excl. VAT/month) | Managed (1,499€ excl. VAT/month) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Commercialisation** (Inbound) | Yes (30% fee) | Yes (30% to 15%*) | Yes (15% fixed) |
| **Advertiser Prospecting** | No | Yes — Database Access | Yes — Delegated (Sales Ops) |
| **Direct Collaboration** (Trade) | No | Yes (0% fees) | Yes (0% fees) |
| **Acquisition Delegation** | No | No | Yes — Included |
| **Withdrawal Fees** | 5% (after 1st) | None | None |
| **Affiliation** | Yes (20% earnings) | Yes (20% earnings) | Yes (20% earnings) |
| **Commitment** | 12 months | 12 months | 12 months |

### * Sliding scale detail (Platform plan)

| Total annual revenue | Network fee |
| :--- | :--- |
| €0 to €5,000 | **30%** |
| €5,000 to €25,000 | **25%** |
| €25,000 to €75,000 | **20%** |
| Over €75,000 | **15%** |

::: tip Withdrawal fees
With the Starter plan, the first annual withdrawal is free, then 5% applies. With Platform and Managed plans, withdrawals are unlimited and free.
:::

::: info Funds availability & Wallet
Revenue is credited to your Wallet **30 days after the campaign ends**, following reporting validation. Payment is made by bank transfer within 30 days end of month upon your request.

The balance can be **reinvested without fees** to fund your own acquisition campaigns.

Any balance not withdrawn after **12 consecutive months of inactivity** is definitively transferred to getinside.
:::

::: info Contractual framework — March 2026 update
Since 1 March 2026, the standalone Starter contract is integrated into the General Terms of Use and Commercialisation (CGUC). The contractual framework is based on four documents: CGUC · CGV · General Referral Terms · Privacy Policy.
:::
