# Contest

The mechanism that combines **awareness**, **qualified lead acquisition**, and **immediate conversion**, thanks to the endorsement of a partner e-retailer.

---

## Available Mechanics

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🎟️ Golden Ticket</strong>
    <p>A winning ticket is inserted directly into the distributor's parcels. The surprise at the moment of unboxing maximizes the emotional impact.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🎡 Wheel of Fortune</strong>
    <p>Fun mechanics activated via a QR code (<em>"Scan to spin the wheel"</em>). High participation rate and natural virality.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🧠 Quiz / Profile</strong>
    <p>Interactive format (<em>"What is your profile?"</em>) that generates exceptional engagement and qualifies leads at the same time.</p>
  </div>
</div>

:::tip Field Example
AJY (wellness brand) achieved a **21.6% scan rate** with a *"Discover your Dosha"* quiz — one of the highest scores observed on the network.
:::

## Why launch a contest?

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>📊 Lead Acquisition (Opt-in)</strong>
    <p>In exchange for their participation, players agree to receive your offers. You enrich your CRM with <strong>consenting and qualified</strong> contacts based on the distributor's purchasing behavior.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>💰 Conversion: no losers</strong>
    <p>Each participant receives a **consolation promo code** (e.g., THANKS15). This transforms a free mechanic into immediate sales — even for those who do not win the grand prize.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>❤️ Strong Engagement</strong>
    <p>Gamification creates a positive emotional bond between your brand and the distributor's audience. You benefit from their credibility with their community.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Your Investment

Apart from the media budget, you provide two elements:

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🏆 The Prize (Grand prizes)</strong>
    <p>The draw product that attracts players. Example: <em>"Win a year of products"</em> or <em>"3 × 100€ in vouchers"</em>. You manage the shipping to the winners after the draw.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">

</div>

## 5-Step Workflow

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">1</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Brief & Offer Definition</h3>
    <p>Via the platform, provide the prizes (grand prize + consolation promo code), the visuals (packshots, HD logo), and select the partner distributor.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">2</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Setup (Distributor)</h3>
    <p>The distributor creates a co-branded landing page on their site and activates their traffic channels (newsletter, banners, web push) to direct their community to the game.</p>
    ::: warning 💡 Hosting & LP Creation
    Hosting and creation of the **Landing Page (LP)** are the exclusive responsibility of the **distributor** on their own domain (e.g., `your-site.com/brand-contest`). This technical proximity to the usual audience maximizes conversion rates and trust.
    :::
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">3</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Opt-in Collection (GDPR)</h3>
    <p>Upon registration, the participant actively checks a box:</p>
    <blockquote><em>☑ I agree to receive commercial offers from the partner [Your Brand Name].</em></blockquote>
    <p>Only participants who have checked this box are transmitted and billed to you.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">4</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Data Transfer & Activation</h3>
    <p>At the end of the campaign, the distributor sends you the secure CSV file of opt-in leads via getinside. Integrate them into your CRM (Mailchimp, Klaviyo...) and <strong>immediately</strong> send the welcome email with the promised promo code — timing is key to maximize conversion.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num done">5</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Draw</h3>
    <p>You draw the grand prize winners and manage the shipping of the rewards directly to them.</p>
  </div>
</div>

:::warning GDPR — mandatory rules
- The opt-in box must **never** be pre-checked.
- Only participants who have voluntarily checked it are transmitted to you.
- getinside provides legal notice templates validated by lawyers.
:::
