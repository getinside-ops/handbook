> **Concept:** The most powerful format. The partner sends an email **100% dedicated** to your brand to their active customer base.
> **Goal:** Customer Acquisition, Qualified Traffic.

---

## 1. What to provide (The Brief)

To launch production, the publisher needs high-quality raw materials.

### A. Visual Assets üé®
| Item | Format | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Logos** | PNG / SVG | **Transparent background** mandatory. Provide both Black & White versions. |
| **Visuals** | JPG / PNG | Provide 3 to 5 HD images. Focus on **Lifestyle** (worn product/context) rather than plain packshots. |

### B. Copywriting ‚úçÔ∏è
* **Subject Line:** < 50 chars. Must be catchy.
* **Pre-header:** The sentence following the subject line.
* **Main Title (H1):** The catchphrase visible on the main visual.
* **Key Message:** 3-4 lines presenting your brand and offer.
* **CTA (Button):** Action verb (e.g., *"Shop now"*, *"Discover the collection"*).

### C. Technical üîó
* **Destination URL:** The Landing Page link.
    * *Note: We handle the tracking link generation.*

---

## 2. Validation Process
1.  **Brief:** You submit the assets.
2.  **Draft:** The publisher creates the email and sends a proof (BAT).
3.  **Validation:** You check texts and links.
4.  **Scheduling:** Once validated, the campaign is locked for sending.

{: .warning }
**Deadline:** Briefs must be sent at least **5 business days** before the launch date.
