# Campaign Workflow

Deploying a newsletter insert is fast. Here are the steps from submission to broadcast.

---

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">1</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Sending creative elements</h3>
    <p>Transmit to the distributor (via the getinside platform):</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>The visual</strong> — a JPG, PNG, or GIF image respecting the <a href="./specs">technical specifications</a>.</li>
      <li><strong>The destination link</strong> — the URL of the landing page where clicks should be redirected.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">2</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Tracking Configuration</h3>
    <p>The getinside team generates a **unique tracked redirection link** (`gtinsi.de` format) and sends it to the distributor. This link counts every click on your banner.</p>
    <p>To measure sales in Google Analytics, add UTM parameters to your final URL:<br /><code>mysite.com?utm_source=getinside&utm_medium=display-email&utm_campaign=spring</code></p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">3</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Integration and Validation (Proof)</h3>
    <p>The distributor integrates your image into their newsletter and sends you a Proof (BAT) for preview.</p>
    <p>Check the rendering — especially on mobile — then validate on the platform.</p>
  </div>
</div>

:::warning Required action to unblock sending
1. Log in to [app.getinside.media](https://app.getinside.media/){target="_blank"}.
2. Open the relevant campaign.
3. Click the **"Validate campaign"** button.

Until you validate, the newsletter will not be sent.
:::

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num done">4</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Broadcast</h3>
    <p>Once both validations are recorded (advertiser + distributor), the newsletter is sent on the scheduled date. Track clicks in real-time from your dashboard.</p>
  </div>
</div>
