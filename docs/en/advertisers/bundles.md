# Bundles & Media Kits

A Bundle is a coordinated campaign that activates multiple inventory channels from a single e-retailer in one operation: parcel insert, email, social, display. Where a standalone campaign creates a single exposure, a Bundle creates a presence effect that multiplies conversion rates.

## How it works

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">1</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Choose your retailer and mechanic</h3>
    <p>Identify a partner in the catalogue via the "Multi-channel Offers" icon. Choose the mechanic based on your objective: promotion, game/contest, brand content, Drive to Store, Drive to App, or feedback.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">2</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Provide your campaign assets</h3>
    <p>Via the platform: visuals (HD logo, packshots), offer or prize, consolation promo code. The retailer creates the co-branded landing page on their domain.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">3</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>The campaign runs across all channels</h3>
    <p>Flyers in parcels, dedicated newsletter, homepage banners, Instagram/TikTok posts. Everything runs in parallel for the duration of the campaign.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num done">4</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Collect leads and track results</h3>
    <p>At close, the retailer sends you the CSV file of opt-ins via getinside. Import them into your CRM and immediately send the welcome email with the promised promo code.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Performance

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>Up to 20% CTR</strong>
    <p>Gamified formats (contests, fortune wheels, quizzes) reach up to 20% click-through rate — vs 2.64% network average for standard parcel inserts.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>Qualified opt-in leads</strong>
    <p>Each consenting participant enriches your CRM with a contact qualified by the retailer's purchasing data. No losers: everyone receives a consolation promo code.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>Stronger brand recall</strong>
    <p>Three exposures (physical + email + social) on the same message create a presence effect that mechanically improves recall and conversion.</p>
  </div>
</div>

::: tip Field example
AJY (wellness brand) achieved a 21.6% scan rate with a "Discover your Dosha" quiz — one of the highest scores observed on the network.
:::

## Available mechanics

| Mechanic | Primary objective |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Promotion** | Direct conversion via promo code or discount voucher |
| **Game/Contest** | Opt-in leads + consolation promo code for everyone |
| **Content / Brand Content** | Awareness and expert positioning |
| **Drive to Store** | Foot traffic to physical locations |
| **Drive to App** | Qualified app installs |
| **Feedback / Rating** | Review collection and consumer panels |

## Pricing

| Item | Cost |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Total media budget** | Package rate — between €10,000 and €15,000 for a full one-month campaign |
| **Platform fee** | 12% of invested media budget |
| **Creative Studio** (optional) | 550€ excl. VAT — Email, Social, Print production (excluding Landing Page) |
| **Prize** | Your responsibility — market value + shipping to winners |

The package price is generally more cost-effective than buying the channels separately. You can adjust the contents of the pack (remove a channel, modify volume) in direct discussion with the retailer.
