# Bundles & Media Kits

The **Bundle** format (also called **Media Kit** or **360° Campaign**) is a comprehensive advertising strategy that allows you to simultaneously activate multiple marketing channels with a single e-commerce partner.

Instead of buying a single channel (like package inserts), you purchase a **scripted campaign** that reaches the retailer's audience at several key moments in their shopping journey.

::: tip KEY CONCEPT
The goal is to create a **red thread** between your brand and the retailer's brand. Repeating the message across different channels (physical and digital) reinforces memorability and **multiplies conversion rates**.
:::

## Why Choose a Bundle?

| Benefit | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **📣 Repetition & Recall** | Reaching the consumer via email, in their package, and on social media creates a reassuring "omnipresence" effect (Mere Exposure Effect). |
| **🚀 Maximized Conversion** | A prospect who sees your offer 3 times via different channels is much more likely to convert than with a single exposure. |
| **🎁 Engagement (Lead Gen)** | Often centered around an integrated game, bundles allow you to collect qualified leads (emails) in addition to direct sales. |
| **💰 Flat Rate Pricing** | Unlike standard CPM, bundles are often sold at a **fixed price (flat fee)**, allowing you to control your acquisition budget. |

## Bundle Mechanics

A Bundle does not necessarily involve a contest. The mechanic is chosen based on your objective and brand nature. Here are the 6 available types:

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🎁 Promotion</strong>
    <p>Direct discount offer, promo code, or voucher. The simplest and most immediately converting mechanic.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🏪 Drive to Store</strong>
    <p>Encourages the audience to visit a partner physical store. Ideal for brands with a retail presence.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>📖 Content / Brand Content</strong>
    <p>Sponsored article, brand storytelling, or educational content. Strengthens brand awareness and expert positioning.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🎮 Contest</strong>
    <p>Opt-in leads + consolation promo code for all participants. Gamified formats reaching up to <strong>20% CTR</strong>.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>📱 Drive to App</strong>
    <p>App download or loyalty program signup. Perfect for boosting qualified installs.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>⭐ Feedback / Rating</strong>
    <p>Customer review collection, NPS, or consumer panels. Generates social proof while enriching your customer knowledge.</p>

## Contest integrated into the Bundle

Among the available mechanics, the contest is often the most powerful for simultaneously generating **awareness**, **qualified leads**, and **immediate conversion**.

### The 3 game formats

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🎟️ Golden Ticket</strong>
    <p>A winning ticket is inserted directly into the distributor's parcels. The surprise at the moment of unboxing maximizes the emotional impact.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🎡 Wheel of Fortune</strong>
    <p>Fun mechanics activated via a QR code (<em>"Scan to spin the wheel"</em>). High participation rate and natural virality.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🧠 Quiz / Profile</strong>
    <p>Interactive format (<em>"What is your profile?"</em>) that generates exceptional engagement and qualifies leads at the same time.</p>
  </div>
</div>

::: tip Field Example
AJY (wellness brand) achieved a **21.6% scan rate** with a *"Discover your Dosha"* quiz — one of the highest scores observed on the network.
:::

### Why run a contest?

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>📊 Lead Acquisition (Opt-in)</strong>
    <p>In exchange for their participation, players agree to receive your offers. You enrich your CRM with <strong>consenting and qualified</strong> contacts based on the distributor's purchasing behavior.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>💰 Conversion: no losers</strong>
    <p>Each participant receives a <strong>consolation promo code</strong> (e.g., THANKS15). This transforms a free mechanic into immediate sales — even for those who do not win the grand prize.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>❤️ Strong Engagement</strong>
    <p>Gamification creates a positive emotional bond between your brand and the distributor's audience. You benefit from their credibility with their community.</p>
  </div>
</div>

### Your Investment

Apart from the media budget, you provide two elements:

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🏆 The Prize (Grand prizes)</strong>
    <p>The draw product that attracts players. Example: <em>"Win a year of products"</em> or <em>"3 × 100€ in vouchers"</em>. You manage the shipping to the winners after the draw.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🎟️ Consolation Reward</strong>
    <p>A generic promo code (e.g., <code>THANKS15</code>) offered to <em>all</em> participants. Simple to prepare, high impact on conversion.</p>
  </div>
</div>

  </div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">3</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">

    <blockquote><em>☑ I agree to receive commercial offers from the partner [Your Brand Name].</em></blockquote>
    <p>Only participants who have checked this box are transmitted and billed to you.</p>

<div class="gi-step">

- The opt-in box must **never** be pre-checked.

:::

::: info CONCRETE EXAMPLE
**The "Healthy Week" Operation:**
A food retailer launches a themed week around the integrated contest.
*   **Monday:** Dedicated email announcing the contest.
*   **Tuesday:** Instagram post relaying the operation.
*   **All week:** Flyers in all packages with a QR code to participate.
*   **Result:** The advertiser collects thousands of emails (opt-in) and generates sales via the consolation promo code.

## Pricing Model

Bundles simplify media buying via a **Flat Fee** approach.

*   **Average Price Observed:** Between **€10,000 and €15,000** for a complete campaign over a given period (e.g., 1 month).
*   **Flexibility:** This rate is generally more advantageous than the sum of the channels purchased separately at CPM.
*   **Negotiation:** It is possible to adjust the content of the pack (e.g., remove a social channel to lower the cost) in direct discussion with the retailer via the platform.
