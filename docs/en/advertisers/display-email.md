> **Concept:** Your brand appears as a "native recommendation" inside the publisher's newsletter.

## 1. Requirements

### Option A: Image Only Banner
* **Dimensions:** 1200 x 600 px (Landscape) OR 1080 x 1080 px (Square).
* **Format:** JPG or PNG (High Quality).
* **Weight:** < 200 KB.

### Option B: Native Block (Image + Text)
* **Visual:** Lifestyle photo without text (600 x 600 px).
* **Title:** 5 to 7 words max.
* **Description:** 1 short sentence.
* **CTA:** Button text (e.g., "Discover").

---

## 2. Best Practices
* **Be Direct:** You have 2 seconds of attention span.
* **Contrast:** Your visual must stand out on a white background.
* **Fake Button:** If using Option A (Image), design a button *inside* the image to encourage clicks.
