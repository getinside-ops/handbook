# Technical Specifications

The constraints to respect to guarantee optimal print quality and high-performance tracking.

---

## Paper Formats

| Format | Finished (cut) | File (with bleed) | Safe zone |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **A5** | 148 × 210 mm | **152 × 214 mm** | 3 mm internal |
| **A6** | 105 × 148 mm | **109 × 152 mm** | 3 mm internal |
| **Card** | 105 × 148 mm | **109 × 152 mm** | See template |

:::warning Bleed: 2 mm mandatory
The material (image or colored background) must extend **2 mm outside** the finished format. Without bleed, a white border appears after cutting.
:::

:::tip Safe zone: 3 mm
No text, logo, or important element should be less than **3 mm from the inner edge**. Anything too close to the edge risks being cropped.
:::

---

## Files & Colorimetry

| Criterion | Specification |
| :--- | :--- |
| **File format** | PDF/X (vector) — preferred. JPG or PNG accepted. |
| **Resolution** | **300 DPI minimum** |
| **Color mode** | **CMYK mandatory** (no RGB) |
| **ICC Profile** | Coated FOGRA39 |
| **Ink coverage** | **Maximum 300%** |

:::tip Tip — Deep Black
For a rich black on solid areas (large black backgrounds): use **C30% M30% Y30% K100%**.
For fine texts and small characters: use **K100% only** to avoid shifting during printing.
:::

---

## Paper

:::info Recommended paper specifications
- **Weight:** 250 to 300 g/m²
- **Type:** Matte coated or semi-matte coated
- **Certification:** FSC or PEFC
- **Lamination:** No — lamination makes QR codes less readable
:::

**Why?** This unique QR code certifies distribution, enables real-time monitoring, and activates your performance dashboard.

## Sample Formats (Sampling)

getinside allows the distribution of samples alone or assembled with a flyer.

| Format | Max weight | Max developed (H + W + L) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Format S** | 15 g | 15 cm |
| **Format M** | 100 g | 30 cm |
| **Format L** | 450 g | 40 cm |

:::info "Duo" Option — Flyer + Sample Assembly (Format S)
The sample is glued to an A5 or A6 flyer via a dot of **fugitive glue** (repositionable), allowing it to be detached without damaging the flyer.

This service is performed by **Adapted Enterprises (EA)**, promoting the employment of disabled workers.
:::

## Proof (BAT) Checklist

To check before each submission:

1. Dimensions include **+2 mm bleed** and respect **-3 mm safe zone**.
2. File is in **CMYK (FOGRA39)**, resolution ≥ **300 DPI**, ink coverage ≤ **300%**.
3. getinside QR code (`.svg`) is present and measures at least **25 × 25 mm**.
4. Universal legal notices are included: advertiser ID, Triman, getinside logo, T&Cs.
5. Mandatory sectorial notices (if applicable) are legible.
