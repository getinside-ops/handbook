## Paper Specifications

| Criterion | Specification |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Formats** | A5 (148 × 210 mm), A6 (105 × 148 mm), Gift card |
| **Weight** | 250 to 300 g/m² |
| **Type** | Matte coated or semi-matte coated |
| **Certification** | FSC or PEFC mandatory |
| **Lamination** | ❌ No — makes QR codes less readable |

## Use Cases

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>📦 For your campaigns (Sponsored Mail)</strong>
    <p>You book a campaign with a partner distributor. getinside prints your materials, manages assembly (e.g., fugitive glue dot for sample), and delivers directly to the distributor's warehouse.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🏷️ Self-promotion (For you)</strong>
    <p>Do you want to print your own thank you cards or flyers to insert into <strong>your own parcels</strong>? Create an "internal" campaign via the platform and benefit from our industrial group rates. Pallet delivery to your door.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Economic Model

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>💎 Transparency</strong>
    <p>The cost is clearly displayed during campaign configuration. No surprises.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>📦 All included</strong>
    <p>Printing + Finishing + Transport. A single item, a single contact.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>📉 Economies of scale</strong>
    <p>The larger the volume, the lower the unit cost. Sliding scale rates apply from the first few thousand copies.</p>
  </div>
</div>

:::tip Just-in-time Delivery
Prints are delivered directly to distributors' warehouses, synchronized to their logistics **Lead Time**. The merchandise arrives exactly when the partner needs it — zero unnecessary storage on your end.
:::

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">

  <div class="gi-value-card">
