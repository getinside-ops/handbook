# Pricing & Budget

getinside is a self-service platform. Free access, transparent model, no commitment.

## Overview

| Item | Cost | Mandatory |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Platform access** | Free | — |
| **Platform fee** | 12% of invested media budget | Yes |
| **Creative Studio** | 550€ excl. VAT per creation | Optional |
| **Printing & Logistics** | Volume-based pricing (all included) | Optional |
| **Dedicated Account Manager** | Free from €5,000 excl. VAT budget | Automatic |

## Platform fee

::: tip What's included in the 12%
- No advertiser subscription — access is free.
- No minimum budget — test with small amounts.
- Access to 250+ exclusive audiences, 300+ active advertisers, 40 million consumers.
- Single centralized invoice regardless of the number of audiences purchased.
:::

::: info Are you also an e-retailer?
If you monetize your parcels or mailings via getinside, your retailer subscription (Starter, Platform, Managed) is separate from your campaign purchases. → [See retailer plans](/publishers/pricing)
:::

::: info When is the invoice generated?
Billing is triggered as soon as you definitively validate the Purchase Order on the platform. No billing before your validation.
:::
