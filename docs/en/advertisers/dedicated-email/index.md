# Dedicated Email (Advertiser)

Broadcast your message exclusively to a qualified audience, carried by the retailer's recommendation. The email comes from a brand the subscriber chose to follow, so you arrive as trusted advice rather than a cold ad.

The principle is simple. You send your brief and visuals; the retailer designs the email in their brand style and sends it to a targeted segment of their base. You validate the proof before sending and track performance in real time.

::: tip For retailers
Are you an e-retailer looking to broadcast this format for your advertisers? See the [Dedicated Email guide for retailers](/en/publishers/dedicated-email/).
:::

## Why this format

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>Retailer endorsement</strong>
    <p>The recommendation comes from a brand the subscriber already follows. You benefit from their customer relationship, not just their audience.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>Open rate above 50%</strong>
    <p>On an engaged post-purchase base, dedicated email regularly exceeds 50% open rate, well above display standards.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>Turnkey production</strong>
    <p>You provide the brief and visuals. The retailer builds the email, with no technical integration on your side.</p>
  </div>
</div>
