# Dedicated Emailing (Dedicated Newsletter)

Communicate exclusively to our partner e-retailers' subscriber base. This format relies on **Endorsement**: the distributor personally recommends you to their community.

:::info Premium Format
Dedicated Emailing offers the highest engagement rates in the media mix (OR > 50% on certain flows).
:::

## Why Dedicated Emailing performs better?

Unlike traditional list renting (Cold Emailing), Dedicated Emailing benefits from the retailer's **moral endorsement**.

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🛡️ Transferred Trust</strong>
    <p>The subscriber receives the email from a brand they know and appreciate. Your offer is perceived as a "member privilege".</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🎯 1st Party Targeting</strong>
    <p>We use real purchase data (product universe, average order value) to broadcast your offer only to affinitive profiles.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>📈 10x Interaction</strong>
    <p>The click-through rate (CTR) is up to 10 times higher than a standard cold acquisition campaign.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Measurement & Double Tracking

getinside ensures total transparency on the performance of your sends.

1. **Platform Reporting:** Open Rate (OR), Click-Through Rate (CTR), Unique Clicks.
2. **Double Tracking:** We track clicks via our `gtinsi.de` links. We systematically recommend adding **UTMs** to reconcile this data with your sales in your Analytics tool.
3. **Post-Campaign Reporting:** A consolidated report is sent to you 7 days after the blast (for Blast campaigns).
