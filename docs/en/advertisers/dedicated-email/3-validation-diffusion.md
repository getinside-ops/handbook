# Step 3: Validation & Broadcast

The creative is complete. Your action is required to unblock sending.

## Validation process

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">1</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Tracking configuration</h3>
    <p>This step is transparent to you. The getinside team and the retailer embed the open pixels and tracked redirection links. Your links remain dynamic — you can modify the destination URL at any time in the <strong>"Configuration and Tracking"</strong> tab, even after validation.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">2</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Receiving the proof (BAT)</h3>
    <p>As soon as the retailer has finalized the email and getinside has technically verified it, you receive an email notification. You can access a <strong>static preview</strong> of the campaign to check compliance with the brief: offer, visuals, wording.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">3</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Your validation — action required</h3>
    <p>If the creative meets your expectations, log in to <a href="https://app.getinside.media/" target="_blank">app.getinside.media</a>, open the relevant campaign, and click <strong>"Validate campaign"</strong>. Until you validate, the campaign remains blocked.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num done">4</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Broadcast</h3>
    <p>Once both validations are recorded (yours and the retailer's), the campaign is scheduled and sent to the targeted audience. Track open and click statistics in real time from your dashboard.</p>
  </div>
</div>

::: tip Resend to non-openers
Some retailers offer to automatically resend the campaign to subscribers who did not open the email during the first send. Request this during the brief to maximize coverage.
:::
