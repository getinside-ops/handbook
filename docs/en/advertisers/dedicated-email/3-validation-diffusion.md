# Step 3: Validation and Broadcast

Creation is complete. Only the Proof (BAT) needs to be validated to authorize sending.

---

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">1</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Tracking Configuration</h3>
    <p>This step is invisible to you. The getinside team and the distributor embed the open pixels and tracked redirection links.</p>
    <p><strong>Reminder:</strong> your links are dynamic — you can change the destination URL at any time in the <strong>"Configuration and Tracking"</strong> tab, even after validation.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">2</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Receiving the Proof (BAT)</h3>
    <p>As soon as the distributor has finalized the email and getinside has technically verified it, you receive an email notification.</p>
    <p>You can access a <strong>static preview</strong> of the campaign to check adherence to the brief (offer, visuals, wording).</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">3</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Your Validation (Action Required)</h3>
    <p>If the creative satisfies you, you must validate on the platform to unblock the sending.</p>
  </div>
</div>

:::warning Required action to unblock sending
1. Log in to [app.getinside.media](https://app.getinside.media/){target="_blank"}.
2. Open the relevant campaign.
3. Click the **"Validate campaign"** button.

Until you validate, the campaign remains blocked — it will not be broadcast.
:::

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num done">4</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>Broadcast</h3>
    <p>Once both validations are recorded (yours + the distributor's), the campaign is scheduled and sent to the targeted audience.</p>
    <p>Track statistics (opens, clicks) in real-time from your dashboard.</p>
  </div>
</div>

:::tip Resend to non-openers
Some distributors offer to automatically resend the campaign to subscribers who did not open the email during the first send. Ask for it during the brief to maximize coverage.
:::
