# Step 1: Advertiser Brief

Your campaign starts here. The brief is the raw material that allows the retailer to design a high-performing email in your brand's style.

## Submit the form

Every Dedicated Email campaign starts with submitting the brief via the dedicated form.

→ **[Access the Brief form (Tally)](https://tally.so/r/9qN9WY){target="_blank"}**

## What you need to provide

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">1</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>The offer</h3>
    <p>Craft a clear and compelling promise: <em>"€20 off from €100 purchase"</em>. Specify the validity period — minimum 3 months recommended.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">2</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>The visuals</h3>
    <p>Vector or HD logo, product packshots on a white background, lifestyle visuals if available.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num">3</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>The wording</h3>
    <p>Key arguments (USPs), brand tone, mandatory keywords to integrate in the email copy.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="gi-step">
  <div class="gi-step-num done">4</div>
  <div class="gi-step-body">
    <h3>The destination links</h3>
    <p>URLs of the landing pages to redirect traffic to. These links are dynamic — you can modify them at any time from the <strong>"Configuration and Tracking"</strong> tab, even after launch.</p>
  </div>
</div>

::: tip Let the retailer adapt your brand universe
Don't try to impose strict brand guidelines. The retailer knows their community — they will adapt your visuals to their tone to maximize engagement. A 100% branded email with no mention of the partner increases unsubscribes and drops performance.
:::

## What's next?

Once the brief is submitted, the getinside team sets up the campaign and notifies the retailer to start production.
