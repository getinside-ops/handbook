# Step 1: The Advertiser Brief

Your role: provide the "raw material" that will allow the distributor to build a high-performing email.

---

## Fill out the form

Every campaign starts with submitting the brief via our dedicated form.

→ **[Access the Brief form (Tally)](https://tally.so/r/9qN9WY){target="_blank"}**

---

## What you need to provide

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🎁 The Offer</strong>
    <p>What is the promise? Be clear and incentive. Example: <em>"20€ off for orders over 100€"</em>.</p>
    <p>Specify the validity period (recommended minimum: 3 months).</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🖼️ The Visuals</strong>
    <p>Vector or HD logo, product packshots on a white background, lifestyle visuals if available.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>✍️ The Wording</strong>
    <p>Key arguments (USPs), brand tone, mandatory keywords to include in the email text.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>🔗 The Links</strong>
    <p>Destination URLs (landing pages) to direct traffic to. These links are dynamic — editable at any time.</p>
  </div>
</div>

:::tip Advice: let the distributor adapt
Do not try to impose strict brand guidelines. The distributor knows their community — let them adapt your visuals to their universe to maximize engagement.

**The goal is a recommendation, not an ad.** A 100% branded email without mentioning the partner increases unsubscribes and drops performance.
:::

---

## What's next?

Once the brief is sent, the getinside team sets up the campaign and notifies the distributor to start creation.
