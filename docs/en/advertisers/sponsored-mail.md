> **Concept:** Your physical asset (Flyer or Sample) is inserted into parcels shipped by our partners.
> **Advantage:** 100% Open Rate. You reach the customer during the Unboxing moment.

## 1. Technical Specs

### The Paper Asset (Flyer)
* **Format:** A6 (Postcard) or A5 max.
* **Weight:** Ideally between 135g and 250g.
* **Texture:** Avoid thin paper that wrinkles easily.

### The Sample (Goodies)
* **Weight:** Generally limited to < 50g.
* **Robustness:** Must withstand transport shocks. No fragile glass or leaking liquids.

## 2. Logistics & Delivery

* **Address:** We will provide the exact warehouse address.
* **Deadline:** Goods must be delivered **10 business days** before the operation starts.
* **Packaging:**
    * Flyers must not be loose.
    * **Labeling:** Each box must be labeled: *"GETINSIDE CAMPAIGN - [YOUR BRAND] - FOR INSERTION"*.
