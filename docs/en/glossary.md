<h2 id="a">A</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Partner Advertiser (Annonceur Partenaire)</dt>
  <dd>Advertiser with whom a Distributor collaborates directly via the platform. Unlike general network advertisers, direct partners benefit from 0% platform fees on Platform or Managed offers.</dd>
  <dt>Prospect Advertiser (Annonceur Prospect)</dt>
  <dd>Commercial target identified or imported by the Distributor to drive their acquisition pipeline for new advertisers (integrated CRM).</dd>
  <dt>Advertiser (Annonceur)</dt>
  <dd>Brand that buys advertising space to promote its products or services.</dd>
  <dt>Sponsored Mail (Insertion Colis)</dt>
  <dd>Marketing technique consisting of inserting an advertising offer (flyer, card, sample) inside the shipping parcel of a partner e-retailer.</dd>
  <dt>Lookalike Audience (1st Party LAL)</dt>
  <dd>Advertising targeting aimed at profiles with similar characteristics (purchasing behavior, demographics) to the distributor's actual buyers, based on their real sales data.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="b">B</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>BAT (Bon à Tirer) / Proof</dt>
  <dd>Final validation of the mockup (digital) before launching production or broadcasting. Once validated, no modification is possible.</dd>
  <dt>Brief</dt>
  <dd>Document filled out by the Advertiser detailing their objectives and providing the graphic elements necessary for the campaign.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="c">C</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Capping</dt>
  <dd>Volume limit imposed on a campaign (e.g. limit to 1 flyer per parcel maximum).</dd>
  <dt>Co-branding</dt>
  <dd>Association of two brands (the Advertiser and the Retailer) in the same communication to strengthen the impact through "endorsement".</dd>
  <dt>CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)</dt>
  <dd>Average cost spent to generate a sale or a lead.</dd>
  <dt>CPM (Cost Per Mille)</dt>
  <dd>Standard unit of measurement. A CPM of 15€ means that the advertiser pays 15€ for 1,000 impressions (parcels sent, emails received).</dd>
  <dt>CTR (Click-Through Rate / Scan Rate)</dt>
  <dd>Percentage of people who interacted with the campaign (email click or QR code scan on a flyer). 2026 network average: 2.64%.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="d">D</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Publisher / Retailer (Distributeur)</dt>
  <dd>E-retailer or Media that monetizes its audience by broadcasting the advertisements of third-party advertisers.</dd>
  <dt>Double Tracking</dt>
  <dd>Redundant measurement system used for emailing and Social Ads, comparing statistics from the network/platform with actual sessions recorded via UTM on the advertiser's site.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="e">E</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Sampling (Echantillonnage)</dt>
  <dd>Free distribution of a product in a reduced format to have it tested directly in the consumer's home.</dd>
  <dt>Eco-design (Eco-conception)</dt>
  <dd>Approach aimed at reducing the environmental impact of materials (e.g., prohibition of plastic lamination to guarantee full recyclability of the paper).</dd>
  <dt>Endorsement</dt>
  <dd>Moral guarantee brought by the Retailer to the Advertiser. The fact that the e-retailer "recommends" the brand via a Partnership Ad or a Dedicated Email boosts trust.</dd>
  <dt>Exchange (Wallet)</dt>
  <dd>Feature allowing a distributor to reinvest their earnings directly into their own acquisition campaigns with 0% management fees.</dd>
  <dt>Wallet Expiration</dt>
  <dd>Management rule: unused or non-withdrawn funds are permanently lost after 12 consecutive months of inactivity (no login AND no movement on the account).</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="f">F</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Annual Forecast</dt>
  <dd>Annual revenue target set by a Distributor for a specific advertiser. Tracked via a visual YTD (Year-To-Date) gauge on the platform.</dd>
  <dt>First Party Data</dt>
  <dd>Behavioral data collected directly by the Retailer. getinside operates as a Trusted Third Party: data never leaves the Retailer's secure environment and is never shared with the advertiser.</dd>
  <dt>FOGRA39</dt>
  <dd>Standard colorimetric profile mandatory for offset printing in Europe.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="l">L</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Landing Page</dt>
  <dd>Specific web page to which the user is redirected after interacting with the offer. Its creation is the advertiser's sole responsibility.</dd>
  <dt>Lead Time (Ingestion Delay)</dt>
  <dd>Incompressible structural logistics delay between the receipt of the materials at the warehouse and their actual insertion into parcels.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="o">O</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Opt-in</dt>
  <dd>Explicit consent of the user to receive commercial communications (mandatory for Dedicated Email and Display Email).</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="p">P</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Partnership Ads</dt>
  <dd>Meta advertising format allowing an advertiser to sponsor a post published from a distributor's account (endorsement effect).</dd>
  <dt>Lamination (Pelliculage)</dt>
  <dd>Addition of a thin layer of matte or glossy plastic on a paper material. Prohibited at getinside to guarantee full recyclability of flyers.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="q">Q</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Dynamic QR Code</dt>
  <dd>2D code whose destination URL can be modified remotely after printing. Must be generated by getinside (.svg).</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="r">R</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Retail Media</dt>
  <dd>Advertising devices present on actual purchasing paths of e-retailers (Parcels, Web, Email, Social).</dd>
  <dt>ROAS (Return On Ad Spend)</dt>
  <dd>Revenue generated for each euro spent on advertising. getinside network average: 4.8.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="s">S</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>SLA (Service Level Agreement)</dt>
  <dd>Reactivity commitment. Distributors commit to responding to any campaign proposal within 48 business hours (subject to penalty).</dd>
  <dt>Distributor Score (Score Distributeur)</dt>
  <dd>Quality rating out of 10 points (displayed as 5 stars) determining a retailer's visibility in the marketplace. Calculated on 7 performance and reliability criteria.</dd>
  <dt>Smart Matching</dt>
  <dd>Algorithm connecting advertisers/audiences integrating the Trust Index (relationship history).</dd>
  <dt>Spark Ads</dt>
  <dd>TikTok equivalent of Partnership Ads.</dd>
  <dt>Studio Fee</dt>
  <dd>Flat fee of 550€ HT for any graphic design service (Print, Email, Social) performed by getinside.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="t">T</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Trust Index</dt>
  <dd>Index from 1 to 5 stars measuring the historical reliability of a relationship between an Advertiser and a specific Distributor.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="u">U</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>UTM</dt>
  <dd>Tracking parameters essential for reconciling getinside clicks with sales in advertiser analytics tools.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="w">W</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Wallet</dt>
  <dd>Virtual account where earnings are credited. Subject to an expiration rule after 12 months of total inactivity.</dd>
</dl>

<hr />

<h2 id="z">Z</h2>
<dl>
  <dt>Safe Zone (Zone Tranquille)</dt>
  <dd>Mandatory 3 mm internal safety margin on Print files (no important text/logo).</dd>
</dl>
