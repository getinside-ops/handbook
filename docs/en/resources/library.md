## 💻 Recommended Software

To prepare your visuals to technical standards, favor the following DTP software:

<div class="gi-value-grid">
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>Adobe Illustrator</strong>
    <p>Creation of vector illustrations. Ideal for exporting in <code>PDF/X</code> with bleed included.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">
    <strong>Adobe Photoshop</strong>
    <p>Raster image manipulation. Verify resolution ≥ 300 DPI and CMYK color mode.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="gi-value-card">

    <p>Multi-page layout with PDF/X export and automatic bleed management.</p>
